although i don't know you

although i don’t know you, i wish i did.
and i’ve got this feeling it would have been great.

although i don’t know you, i struggle to keep you off my mind.
and i wonder if i’m even on yours.

although i don’t know you, it hurts to imagine you with someone who will.
and it’s sad to think of what might have been.

although i don’t know you, the color blue is overwhelming.
but i’ve never seen a shade i didn’t like.
